Accessibility

We are committed to making our website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.

Standards Compliance

Our website strives to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA.

Features for Accessibility

  • Keyboard navigable menus
  • Alt text for images
  • Proper use of headings
  • Contrast ratio for readability

Feedback

If you have any feedback or suggestions on how we can improve the accessibility of our website, please contact us at info@elementsia.gov.
